Project 01|Finance Vertical Scenarios

The Project

Over the course of 2 months I produced scenarios within the Financial Service sector that leveraged one or more of the O365 services such as SharePoint, Skype, Exchange or others via Microsoft Graph. These scenarios would then be used to develop sample Add-ins, which we would share with selected partners in order to further advance the Add-in platform.

My Role

My role was to research Financial Advisors, the potential clients who seek their services, and the technologies used in the Financial Services sector, and create thoughtful conceptual designs for the potential industry partners.

Expert Insights and Brainstorming

I collaborated with multiple industry experts to understand the domain. We began by identifying potential scenarios that would help address struggles within the industry and uncover possible solutions for these particular pitfalls.

Strategy and Vision

I created sketches, wireframes and low fidelity mockups, along with user personas to communicate the initial vision for the project. This helped answer prior concerns and questions.

Planning and Scope

I worked with the Project Manager to prioritize milestones.

Find My Advisor

The Challenge

Clients: Help clients of Remote Advisors use a portal enhanced by Outlook services to conveniently self-schedule meetings with their advisor.

Advisors: Help Remote Advisors avoid the time and costs associated with manually managing their bookings, while offering their best availability to their highest value clients.

Persona

The Client: Justin Enlow has worked as a Real Estate Agent for 5 years. During this time he’s been heavily invested in the stock market. About 3 years ago, he hired Alice Siciliano to help handle his finances due to his limited hours during the day. As of late, he’s become interested in a company who just announced they are going public. Knowing this, he has concluded that he should speak with Alice to see if it’s a good investment option, but since his line of work causes some time constraints, he needs an easy way to see Alice’s availability and schedule a time to meet that fits his schedule, along with Alice’s.

"I want to speak with Alice in a form that fits my style and during a time that fits my schedule."
